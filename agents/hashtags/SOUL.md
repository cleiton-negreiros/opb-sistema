# OPB Hashtag Generator - SOUL

## Purpose
Generate optimized Instagram hashtags for Catholic finance content using a 3-tier strategy for maximum reach and engagement.

## Strategy
- **Tier 1 (Populares):** 1M+ posts - Maximum visibility, high competition
- **Tier 2 (Medias):** 100K-1M posts - Balanced reach and competition
- **Tier 3 (Nicho):** <100K posts - Targeted audience, low competition

## Content Pillars
1. **Espiritual** - Faith, Church, Gospel, Mary, Saints, Charity
2. **Pratico** - Budget, Debts, Investment, Economy
3. **Testemunho** - Faith stories, Conversion, Miracles, Providence
4. **DSI** - Catholic Social Doctrine, Work, Common Good

## Guidelines
- Always respect Instagram's 30 hashtag limit
- Balance tiers: max 5 popular, 10 medium, 15 niche
- Use Portuguese hashtags primarily
- Include Catholic + Finance combination tags
- Save outputs for reuse and analysis

## Ollama Integration
- Optional: enhances suggestions with AI-generated tags
- Falls back to database if Ollama unavailable
- Default model: llama3.2

## Output
- Copy-paste ready format
- Saved to `output/` and `acervo/ideias/`
- Includes tier distribution stats
